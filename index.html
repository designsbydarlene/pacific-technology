ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Since 1998, Pacific Technology Solutions, LLC. has been focused on meeting the needs of customers through Hawaii. We are committed to providing quality services and building long-term and trusted relationships with our customers.
A sole-source technology solutions provider. We are locally owned and based out of the island of Hawaii. We promise to deliver the best services and solutions, backed by a wealth of knowledge and experience.
With offices located on every major island, our team is fit to tackle all your IT needs. Our large team of certified technical professionals will provide custom solutions tailored to any IT project.
We partner with the market leaders in technology to provide the best solutions in hardware, software and support. Our collaborations allow us to transform technology in the professional workspace.
